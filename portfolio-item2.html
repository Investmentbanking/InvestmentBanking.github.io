To Do list app

created with MERN stack

ToDoApp is a dynamic and colorful task management application designed to help you organize your tasks effectively. With a focus on both functionality and user experience, the app allows users to effortlessly manage their daily tasks.

Key features

  • Create New Tasks: Easily add new tasks to your list with just a few clicks. The intuitive interface ensures that adding tasks is a quick and hassle-free process, keeping you focused on what matters.
  • Delete Tasks: Keep your task list clean and up-to-date by removing completed or unnecessary tasks. The delete functionality ensures that your task list only contains whats important.
  • Mark Tasks as Complete: Stay on top of your progress by marking tasks as complete. When a task is marked as done, an arrow strikes through it, visually distinguishing completed tasks from the ones still pending.
  • Colorful and Engaging Design: The apps vibrant color scheme not only makes it visually appealing but also enhances usability. Colors are used strategically to make different functionalities clear, helping you navigate the app with ease and making task management a more enjoyable experience.
